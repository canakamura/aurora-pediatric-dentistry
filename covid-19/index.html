COVID-19



To our valued patients,

Under the guidance of the CDC and the Colorado Dental Association, we assure you that the safety of our dental families and team is our top priority.

♥ In order to check-in, please call our office (720-886-0808) and the front desk will check you in.

♥ Please remain in your car and we will call you back when we are ready to have you seated.

♥ A new COVID-19 Waiver form and questionnaire will be given to you to read and sign.

♥ We will update your insurance and personal information during this call, if needed.

♥ We ask that only one parent/guardian accompany the patient to the appointment. Other siblings are respectfully asked to stay home.

♥ All parents must wear a mask to appointments.

♥ Once you are called to enter, you will be greeted in the waiting area and your temperatures will be taken. Anyone with a temperature over 100.4 degrees will not be seen.

Thank you for being a part of our Aurora Pediatric Dentistry family. We are happy to answer any questions you may have about the steps we are taking to keep our patients safe. To contact us, please call our office at (720) 886-0808.
